About us

Medea Lab

Medea Lab, Malmö University, is a transdisciplinary collective of researchers and artists who address cultural and societal challenges through experiments, installations and interventions. Established in 2009, recent projects have dealt with topics such as interstellar space exploration, artificial creativity, the politics of listening, hauntology, collaborative song writing, and transmedia storytelling.

Medea Lab produces public lectures with researchers and artists in the Medea Talks series. More than 50 lectures have been given, and most of them are available to take part of online. Medea Lab also produces a podcast series: Medea Vox.

https://mau.se/en/medea
