Background

The Voyager

For more than 40 years Voyager 1 and Voyager 2 have travelled through space transmitting data to Earth. They have now left our Solar System, the only spacecraft ever to do so, continuing their intergalactic journey with increasingly infrequent contact to Earth. Voyager’s journeys through outer space may last for another three billion years; continuing long after our planet has been absorbed by the sun.
